Accommodation

Accommodation is NOT included in the cost of this event but can be reserved through the registration page.
During the conference, a bus will operate between Tsukuba Center/Kenkyu-gakuen and the venue.

Some accommodation options:

Hotels at Tsukuba Center

Hotels in Tsukuba Center are recommended for maximum convenience during your stay. Tsukuba Center is located immediately adjacent to Tsukuba Station.
Tsukuba Center (Place "D" in Map) has excellent transport links to/from Tokyo.

Some hotels are listed below. Reservation can be on the hotels' web sites.
During the conference, a bus will operate between Tsukuba Center/Kenkyu-gakuen and the venue.
