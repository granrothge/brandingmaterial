Post Deadline Posters are still being accepted!!

The deadline is 26th Aug, but poster abstracts are still being accepted. The final deadline is 8 September, 2014 9:00(JST;GMT+0900) which is same date the registration deadline.

Call for Abstracts

Abstracts for oral or poster presentations at NOBUGS2014 are now being accepted. The deadline for submission of abstracts is 26 August, 2014 9:00(JST;GMT+0900).

Authors are asked to submit abstracts using the template that is downloadable here. Accepted abstracts will be available online and distributed at the conference.

Guidelines

General
  • Abstracts can only be submitted via the online Registration and Abstract Submission Page.
  • Please note that there is no facility for accepting hard copies of abstracts either by mail or facsimile.
  • Abstracts will be accepted during the period 16 July - 18 August 2014 9:00(JST)
Language of Submission
  • All abstracts must be submitted in English with accurate grammar and spelling suitable for publication.
Abstract Preparation
  • You must use the supplied template when preparing your abstract.
  • Only PDF format is acceptable for abstract submission.
  • The submitted file will be used without any further editing.
Abstract template
  • Word format (.docx)
  • Online submission accepts PDF format ONLY.
Abstract Submission Limit
  • There is no limit on the number of abstracts that may be submitted by any one person.
